Dear Balqis,

My name is Nur Balqis Binti Ridzuan, I am 20 years old and was born in Puchong, Selangor on 15 of August 2000. Currently living in Cyberjaya, Selangor with family and it has been over 2 years since we moved from Puchong. I am a Library Management student, currently studying in UiTM Kedah, on my last semester of Diploma.

As I was born in Puchong, Selangor, as the first child, my parents and I were living in a small home, enough for all 3 of us. On 2002, we moved to Sungai Petani, Kedah as we were living at Laguna Merbok, coincidentally, a town where I am currently studying at. We lived there for over 3 years, after my younger brother was born, we moved back to Puchong, Selangor as my dad bought the first house that is big enough for the family that is growing. I started my kindergarten, and my first elementary school was at SK Puchong Utama 1. I considered myself living in a 'circle', as my second elementary school (which I moved when I was in my last year), at SK Puchong Utama 2, as I decided it on my own, because I was embarrassed on my Standard 5 results and disappoint my parents. Finally, after went for a journey in elementary school, I went to SMK Puchong Utama 1, and eventually, on the same situation, but different year, on my last year as a highschool student, my dad was transferred to Johor. Our whole family moved to Pasir Gudang, Johor as I was Form 5, sadly accepting the fate that I have to face my last year in a new school. My last school was in SMK Tanjung Puteri Resort. As I was born and living in a city in my entire life, this place feels so odd initially. However, after successfully adapting on this new environment, I feel much better and happier with my new friends and home.

Finally, after living nearly 2 years at Johor, we went back to Puchong and after having and living in a home in Puchong over a decade and a half, my parents decided that as their children are growing up, it is their time on letting go the house as we started our new life in Cyberjaya, Selangor.
As for me, after going through SPM, I have received an offer in furthering on Diploma in Library Management in UiTM Kedah. Honestly, I never felt way more heartbroken, as I am dreaming in furthering my studies in Law. Fortunately, after nearly 2 years, now, I have never been more grateful on my decision. As I am currently on my last semester, I have been planning on pursuing my studies on Library Science soon.

Lastly, if you are curious what kind of person I am? Well, I am a friendly person as I tend to approach on everyone easily. I am able to adapt on anyone even each of them having different behaviours. My friends tend to see as someone who pretty much hyper and a happy-go-lucky type of person. My current classmates do see me as someone who overdressed on every single day, yes I do enjoy fashion and makeup genuinely. Back then, I was timid in presentation or even communicating with teachers as most of them do not even know me well. Now, I am much comfortable in communicating and presenting infront of classes and lecturers. I find myself improving day by day. From a teenage girl who went through challenges a long with a sigh constantly, and now a young adult who has accepted her fate along with a smile constantly.

This is me, Balqis.
